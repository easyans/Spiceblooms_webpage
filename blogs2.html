Mustard Seed's Spark

Awaken Your Culinary Destiny

Warm coriander and cumin with hints of black pepper

Meet Mustard Seed's Spark—a bold, tangy blend where mustard seeds take center stage, backed by coriander’s citrusy notes and cumin’s smokiness. Ideal for tempering dals, pickling, or rubbing onto meats before grilling, this mix brings a pungent kick that awakens the palate.

Try it in salad dressings or roasted veggies for a South Indian twist. A little goes a long way!
