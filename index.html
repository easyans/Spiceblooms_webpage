Our Signature Powders & Mixes

Discover the authentic Andhra flavors and easy recipes that make every meal special.

🧄

Kandi Podi – Andhra Protein Punch

A roasted toor dal blend packed with protein, this nutty, earthy powder is a pantry staple. Perfect for mixing with hot rice and ghee for a quick, filling meal.

Recipe – Kandi Podi Rice:
  1. Add 2-3 spoons of Kandi Podi over hot steamed rice.
  2. Top with a spoon of ghee.
  3. Mix well and enjoy with raw onion or pickle.
🌿

Dhanya Podi – Fragrant Instant Mix

This cumin and red chilli blend is light, aromatic, and perfect for daily meals. It adds a spicy-smooth touch to your plate.

Recipe – Dhanya Podi Rice:
  1. Mix 2 spoons with hot rice.
  2. Add a dash of ghee.
  3. Tastes great with papad or curd.
🌱

Pesara Podi – Light & Nourishing

Made from green gram (moong dal), this podi is easy to digest, protein-rich, and ideal for kids, elders, or light meals.

Recipe – Pesara Podi Rice:
  1. Mix 2-3 spoons in hot rice with ghee.
  2. Pair with mango pickle or dry curry for a simple meal.
🌶️

Masala Karam – Spicy Andhra Chilli Mix

A bold blend of dry red chillies, garlic, and spices — this karam elevates every dish from dosas to curries.

Recipe – Masala Karam Dosa:
  1. Sprinkle Masala Karam on dosa while cooking.
  2. Add a little oil and roast it crisp.
  3. Serve with chutney or sambar.
Other Uses:
  • Add to curries for extra spice
  • Sprinkle on stir-fries or idli
✨

Turmeric Powder – Pure Haldi from Andhra

Sourced from local farms, naturally dried, and finely ground — this turmeric is vibrant, aromatic, and perfect for cooking and health drinks.

Uses:
  • Add ½ tsp to dal, curry, or sabzi
  • Mix with warm milk for immunity
  • Apply with honey as a natural face mask
