Chili's Fire

Awaken Your Culinary Destiny

Warm coriander and cumin with hints of black pepper

For those who crave heat, Chili's Fire balances fiery red chilies with the earthy trio of coriander, cumin, and black pepper. It’s a versatile powerhouse—stir into yogurt for dips, dust over popcorn, or blend into marinades for grilled meats.

The warmth builds slowly, letting the spices shine without overwhelming. Perfect for biryanis, chutneys, or even cheesy snacks!
